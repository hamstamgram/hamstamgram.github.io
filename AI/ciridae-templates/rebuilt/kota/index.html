How we
get there

A clear, collaborative process from discovery to delivery — no smoke, no mirrors.

01

Discovery & Strategy

We spend time understanding your business, audience, and ambitions. We audit your existing presence, research your competitors, and define clear success criteria before a single pixel is drawn.

02

Brand & Creative Direction

We establish visual direction through moodboards and concept explorations — deciding on tone, typography, colour, and motion before committing to full design. Fast to align. Zero wasted sprints.

03

Design & Prototype

High-fidelity designs built in Figma. Interactive prototypes you can click through and test with real users. Animations specified. Responsive breakpoints documented. Everything ready for a smooth handoff.

Build & Launch

FAQ's
How much does web design and development typically cost?

The majority of our projects sit between £30k and £150k, but project costs will depend on the final scope of work and vary from project to project. We generally have a minimum project size of £25k and prefer to agree a fixed cost with agreed milestone payments.

How long does a project usually take?

A web design and build project can last around 12–14 weeks, whereas brand-into-website-and-marketing can take 6 months or more. Expect clear milestones and a timeline that respects your urgency, without ever skimping on quality.

Can you accommodate tight deadlines?

Sure thing! We're ready to mobilise our design and development teams, prioritising the essentials to turn your dream site from a fast-approaching deadline into a reality. Flexibility and focus are our middle names.

What ongoing support and maintenance do you offer?

Think of us as the guardians of your digital home. We offer a suite of post-launch support and maintenance services to ensure your website continues to thrive — from regular updates to performance tuning, we keep your site smooth, secure, and evolving with your brand.

How do you handle revisions and feedback?

Your feedback is the secret sauce in our design process. We welcome your thoughts, tweaks, and "aha" moments, incorporating them into each iteration to ensure the end product is nothing short of perfection.

What makes CIRIDAE different from other agencies?

We're a small team that punches above our weight. We combine strategic brand thinking with pixel-perfect craft, and we actually care about your results — not just your approval. We track what happens after launch because beautiful design that doesn't perform isn't good enough for us.

Do you work with startups or only established businesses?

Both. We love early-stage companies building something bold — there's nothing better than helping a founder's vision come to life from scratch. We also enjoy evolving the identity and digital presence of established brands who know they need to level up. What matters is that you're ambitious and ready to invest properly in the outcome.

Which CMS or tech stack do you build on?

We're stack-agnostic but have deep expertise in Next.js, Webflow, and WordPress with headless setups. For SaaS products we work in Figma → Next.js / React. We'll recommend the right stack for your team's ability to manage it, not just what's fashionable. The goal is a site your team can actually own after launch.

How do we get started?

Hit the "Hire us" button or drop us a line at hello@ciridae.com. We'll set up a short discovery call to understand your goals, timeline, and budget. From there we'll put together a tailored proposal. No templates, no generic packages — just a clear plan built around your specific challenges.
