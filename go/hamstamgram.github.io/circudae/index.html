Frequently Asked

Common questions answered

How long does a website take to build?

+

Typically 4-6 weeks for a standard website. We start with discovery and strategy, then move into design and development. Complex projects with custom features may take longer. We keep you updated every step of the way.

Do you handle hosting?

+

Yes. We set up and manage fast, secure hosting on your behalf. It's included in the package. You don't need to worry about server management, backups, or security updates.

What's included in ongoing management?

+

Content updates, security patches, performance monitoring, analytics review, and rank tracking. We also provide recommendations for improvements based on your data. It's designed to keep your website fresh and effective.

Can you migrate my existing website?

+

Absolutely. We can migrate your content, preserve your SEO rankings, and improve the design at the same time. Migration is included in our project scope with no additional cost.

Will my website rank in Google?

+

We build SEO into every site from day one. On-page optimization, technical SEO, and content strategy are core. Ranking depends on competition and effort, but our track record speaks for itself. Most clients see results within 3-6 months.
